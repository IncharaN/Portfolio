About Me!

The first step to become more creative, is by embodying the popular business cliche 'Thinking out of the box'. This cliche means to not think in a cliche manner (ironic right?). I chose to name my blog 'Out of the box' for two reasons.

1. I am an innovator and an aspiring entreprenur. I love embarking on new research topics and creating projects that help people. To do what I do, I need to step out of the box and learn thinking skills. Additionally, as a technology and buisiness lover, I have come to the understanding that these industries progress with innovations. Innovations are only possible with novel thinking, in other words, thinking outside of the box.

2. I recently heard a quote by Deepak Chopra, who said, "Instead of thinking outside of the box, get rid of the box." I hope, as my journey progresses, I will be able to discard this invisible box that constrains me and achieve the limitless ability to achieve all of my dreams. However, before I can throw this box and its constraining chains of me, I need to first step out of the box.
